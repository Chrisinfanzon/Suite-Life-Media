Our Story

We’re Chris and Amanda, a husband-and-wife team with a shared passion for two things: digital excellence and unforgettable travel.

Chris comes from the world of big-brand digital advertising, crafting strategies for global names like Google, Johnson & Johnson, and Applebee's. His passion is making media actually work. He saw too many agencies chasing vanity metrics and awards while forgetting the one thing that matters: driving real-world results. His focus is on performance—using smart, data-driven advertising and the power of AI to help savvy businesses compete with the giants, without needing giant budgets. He believes AI enhances, not replaces, the strategic mind.

Amanda is the voice and the vibe. While Chris builds the engine, she steers the brand. With a natural instinct for social media and cultural trends, she helped build the brands for our own passion projects, from books to podcasts. She understands how to connect with an audience, creating a brand identity that feels authentic and resonates on a human level.

As avid travelers ourselves, we've always been drawn to the hotels that go the extra mile—the ones with a distinct personality and a genuine commitment to hospitality. We know that special feeling a great hotel provides, and we believe that story deserves to be told. So we combined our skills to do just that, helping unique hotels and rentals connect with modern travelers who are looking for more than just a room, but an experience.
